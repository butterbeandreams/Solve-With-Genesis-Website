What I Do

I speak both languages.

Executives think in outcomes. Engineers think in solutions. Most advisors are fluent in one. Here's the same risk, said both ways — an actual example from a site security review.

Engineering says

"Patch cadence on the SCADA historian is 45 days behind policy. Three HMI nodes carry unremediated high-severity CVEs, with no compensating network segmentation."

Boardroom hears

"This gap carries roughly a 1-in-8 chance of a generation outage this year, at an estimated $4M in lost output. Closing it costs $180K and six weeks."
